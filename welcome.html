Welcome to Shopping Wishlist!

Thank you for installing Shopping Wishlist. This extension helps you save products you find while browsing the web to a personal wishlist for later reference.

How to Use

Click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar to open the wishlist popup. You can:

  • Add the current page to your wishlist with one click
  • Manually enter product details
  • View all your saved items
  • Organize items by category
  • Export your wishlist data

Key Features

  • Automatic Information Extraction: The extension attempts to find product prices and descriptions automatically
  • Organization: Sort, filter, and categorize your wishlist items
  • Data Management: Export your wishlist as CSV or JSON, or import existing data
  • Cross-device Sync: Your wishlist syncs across all your devices where you're signed into Chrome

Pro Tip: For the best experience, use the extension on product pages from major retailers. The automatic information extraction works best on standard e-commerce sites.
